Employee Attendance
Recording System

Accurate attendance. Zero ambiguity.

EARS is a unified digital system for managing employee attendance, leave, shifts, and access in a structured and auditable way. It connects employees, HODs, and management on a single platform, ensuring attendance data is accurate, transparent, and consistently enforced. Built for organisations that need attendance records they can actually rely on.

Role-Based Employee Portal

EARS includes a secure employee portal with clearly defined access levels

Administrator /
Superuser

Full system access, configuration, & control

Employee /
Normal User

View attendance, apply for leave, review records

HOD /
Approval User

Review attendance, approve or reject leave and mis-punch requests for assigned teams

This ensures accountability at every level without overexposing system controls.

Attendance, Leave & Shift Management

All changes are logged, traceable, and reflected consistently across reports.

EARS supports structured attendance management through:

Leave application, credit, debit, & approval workflows

Shift and shift pattern configuration

Compensatory off (COFF) and holiday handling

Manual punch entry with approval tracking

EARS is commonly deployed in

Manufacturing units
Corporate and administrative offices
Multi-location enterprises
Shift-based and time-sensitive operations

Biometric Data
Capture & Validation

EARS integrates with biometric devices to ensure attendance data is authentic.

Employee fingerprint and face data is captured securely
