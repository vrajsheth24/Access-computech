What is the ePayPOS600?

The ePayPOS600 is a compact, lightweight payment device that works with your smartphone or tablet. It connects via Bluetooth and allows you to accept payments through chip-and-PIN, tap-and-pay via NFC and magnetic-strip cards.

It’s not overloaded with features you won’t use, it’s built for businesses that need straightforward functionality without the hassle of bulky systems.
