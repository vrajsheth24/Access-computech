Access Computech Among India’s Top Payment Services 2024

Recognised by Finance Outlook India, Access Computech earns its place among the 10 most promising payment & merchant services companies for 2024.

Access Computech Pvt. Ltd. (ACPL) has, since its inception, always been a name synonymous with innovation and reliability in the payment solutions industry. This year, the organisation’s relentless pursuit of excellence has earned a coveted spot on Finance Outlook India’s prestigious list of the “10 Most Promising Payment & Merchant Services - 2024.”

Alongside this accolade, ACPL was also spotlighted in Finance Outlook India’s latest feature article, recognising groundbreaking contributions to democratising payment technology across India.

This achievement marks a milestone in ACPL’s journey—a journey which has been fueled by a very clear vision to empower the micro and small-scale businesses while championing India’s ‘Make in India’ initiative.

A Recognition of Innovation and Purpose

The certificate from Finance Outlook India isn’t just a feather in ACPL’s cap; it is a testament to the company’s commitment to breaking barriers in payment technology. ACPL has made a significant impact by addressing a critical need: affordable, efficient, and accessible payment devices tailored for India’s diverse market. From bustling urban retail spaces in malls to quiet and quaint kirana stores in rural neighborhoods, these devices by ACPL are transforming how businesses accept payments.

ACPL is committed to extending the reach of card payment devices beyond large retail chains and branded outlets to small neighborhood shops.

Ajay Sinha
Founder and Managing Director of ACPL
Innovating for India’s Merchants

In a market dominated by cost-sensitive consumers, ACPL stands out by delivering cutting-edge technology at competitive prices. The product lineup includes the A20 FP and F360, devices specifically engineered to meet the unique demands of Indian merchants.

  • A20 FP: A biometric-enabled payment solution, seamlessly integrated with the Aadhaar ecosystem, catering to rural and metropolitan markets alike.
  • F360: An all-in-one device featuring NFC payment, QR code functionality, and a sound box. This compact powerhouse also won the Red Dot Award for its design and technological innovation.

These devices represent ACPL’s philosophy of combining affordability with advanced functionality, a strategy that has struck a chord with small businesses looking to modernise their payment systems.

Apart from the A20FP and F360, Access Computech also offers an impressive portfolio of adaptive payment devices tailored to various business needs.

Their lineup includes the F310 All-in-One Terminal, a modular NFC-enabled device with options like thermal printers, fingerprint scanners, and barcode readers, ensuring smooth integration into any business environment.

Compact solutions like the F210, epay-POS 600, and F300 stand out with user-friendly touchscreens and support for multiple payment modes, including NFC, magnetic stripe, and QR code scanning. For automated and secure payment processing, devices like the M200 Android POS and M300/M500 ECR streamline operations with ease.

Complementing these are the M10 and M20 sound solutions, known for clear audio alerts and long battery life, making them perfect for businesses focused on efficiency and customer satisfaction.

The Power of Recognition

Awards like this aren’t just about trophies and certificates—they are about market-validation. For ACPL, being named one of India’s most promising companies is a reflection of the company’s enduring impact. It highlights their role in fostering financial inclusion, especially in underserved sectors.

What This Means for the Future

As the payments industry evolves, so does ACPL. The company’s focus on localised manufacturing not only aligns with India’s self-reliance goals but also promises better support and innovation. Beyond payment terminals, ACPL is venturing into complementary products like sound boxes, ensuring they remain at the forefront of market trends.

The recognition from Finance Outlook India serves as a reminder of what ACPL has achieved and the heights they are yet to scale. For their customers, partners, and the industry at large, it signals that Access Computech is more than just a payment solutions provider—they are a trailblazer shaping the future of financial technology.

As ACPL continues its journey, the company’s focus remains steadfast: creating technology that is not only innovative but also accessible, ensuring that businesses of every size can continue to adapt, grow, and thrive in this increasingly digital world.
