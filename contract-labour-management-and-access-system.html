Contract Labour
Management & Access System

Control contract labour access. Stay compliant. Stay audit-ready.

CLMS is a unified digital system for managing the complete lifecycle of contract labour, from registration and compliance documentation to site access and ongoing oversight. It enables organisations to manage contractors and workers systematically while maintaining regulatory compliance, controlled access, and full operational visibility.

Contractor & Worker Management

CLMS provides dedicated portals for all stakeholders

Contractor Portal

  • Worker registration
  • Work order submission
  • Compliance document uploads
  • EPN requests and renewals
  • Sub-contractor management

Management Portal

  • Approval workflows
  • Master data control
  • Contractor & work order monitoring
  • Pass issuance & access governance
  • Reporting and oversight

Biometric Identity & Access Control

CLMS integrates biometric verification to ensure only authorised and approved workers gain site access.
