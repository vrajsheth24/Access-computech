SmartPay & PayNext

L3-Certified Payment Applications for our POS Terminals

SmartPay and PayNext provide L3-certified Android payment applications running on Access Computech POS devices. These solutions enable fast rollout of secure card-present and mobile payments while ensuring compliance with card schemes and regulatory standards. Built to reduce project risk, speed up merchant onboarding, and deliver a seamless transaction experience.

What SmartPay
& PayNext Do

These payment applications sit on top of EMV L1/L2 and PCI-certified hardware to deliver a complete, field-proven payment solution:

Accept credit/debit cards, contactless payments, and mobile wallets

Integrate with Android POS, handheld devices, and desktop POS systems

Support app-based payments, smartphone POS, and ECR integrations

Enable value-added services like dynamic QR, tokenised payments, recurring billing, loyalty, EMI, and offers

Key Features

L3-Certified Payment Apps: Certified with key card schemes to reduce risk and accelerate go-live.

Ready On Our Devices: Pre-integrated on Android and Linux POS devices, supporting chip, magstripe, NFC, and PIN entry.

Rapid Time-to-Market: Faster merchant onboarding using pre-certified hardware and application combinations.

Value-Added Services Ready: Supports dynamic QR, tokenised payments, loyalty programs, and recurring billing.

Where SmartPay & PayNext Excel

Banks and payment aggregators deploying large merchant networks
Projects requiring certified, ready-to-deploy payment stacks
Environments where reliability, speed, & regulatory compliance are critical
