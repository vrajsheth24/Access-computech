F360: A Smart Payment Solution for Modern Businesses

The F360 is here to simplify payments further. It’s quick, secure, and suitable for all types of transactions!

Need a better way to handle payments? 360 is a portable, versatile device that accepts multiple payment methods with ease.

Handling payments can be a challenge for businesses, especially when customers expect convenience and speed. Some want to tap their cards, others prefer scanning a QR code; the variety is endless. For businesses, this is both a blessing and a challenge.

The blessing is customers are more likely to spend when they can pay easily and the challenge is not all businesses have the tools to handle every payment type. Traditional point-of-sale systems are often bulky, expensive, and limited in features. They work fine for large businesses, but what about small shops, pop-up stores, or mobile vendors?

That’s where the F360 comes in. This compact, Red Dot award-winning device makes it easy to accept multiple types of payments. It’s portable, lightweight, and packed with features designed to keep your business running smoothly. Whether you’re running a shop, a food stall, or an event stand, the F360 helps you stay one step ahead.

Features You’ll Love

The F360 is more than just a payment device, it’s a smart solution that solves common payment problems.

Here’s what sets it apart:

  • Compact and Portable

    Big, bulky machines are a thing of the past. The F360 is small, lightweight, and easy to carry. The device is sturdy and built to handle the daily grind. Whether you’re moving between customers at a market or working from a tiny counter, this device is made to fit right in so you can rely on it even during your busiest days.

  • Multiple Payment Options

    This device makes it easy for customers to pay the way they want.

    It supports:

    • Tap-to-pay for contactless card payments.
    • Chip-enabled card payments with simple insert-and-verify functionality.
    • QR code scanning for quick mobile payments.

    By offering all these options, you ensure no customer walks away because of limited payment methods.

  • Clear Payment Alerts

    In a noisy environment like a market or event, it’s hard to keep track of payments. Did the transaction go through? Do you need to retry? The F360 eliminates these doubts with its built-in sound system.

    Every successful payment is confirmed with a clear audio alert, so you and your customer know the transaction was completed. It’s a simple feature, but it makes a big difference.

  • Easy to Connect

    The F360 isn’t just a payment device, it’s part of your setup.

    It connects to:

    • Printers, for generating receipts instantly.
    • Power banks, so you can keep it charged all day, even if you’re on the move.
    • Speakers, if you need louder payment alerts in busy or noisy environments.

    This makes it flexible for different types of businesses, whether you’re on the move or working from one location.

  • Simple and Reliable

    Complicated technology can slow you down. The F360 keeps things simple. There’s no steep learning curve or complex setup, just turn it on, connect it, and start accepting payments. It’s designed to make your life easier, not harder.

How the F360 Fits into Everyday Business

  • Shops

    If you’re running a small shop with limited space, the F360 is perfect. It’s compact and doesn’t clutter your counter, making it easy to handle customers quickly.

  • Mobile Vendors

    For food trucks and other mobile businesses, the portability of the F360 is a huge advantage. You can keep it running all day by pairing it with a power bank, so you never have to stop taking payments.

  • Events and Pop-Up Stores

    Busy events need fast payment solutions. The F360 lets you process transactions quickly and gives instant audio confirmations, so you can serve more customers without slowing down.

Why the F360 Is a Great Choice

The 360 stands out because it combines practicality with thoughtful design. It’s portable, versatile and simple.

Whether you’re running a brick-and-mortar store or a mobile business, the F360 adapts to your needs. It’s not just a payment device, it’s a tool to help you run your business better.

Ready to Simplify Payments?

If you’re looking for a payment solution that’s reliable, portable, and easy to use, the 360 is the answer. It’s built to keep up with modern business needs, offering multiple payment options and easy connectivity.

Check out the F360 today at Access Computech and see how it can transform the way you handle payments. With its award-winning design and practical features, it’s a device that works as hard as you do.
