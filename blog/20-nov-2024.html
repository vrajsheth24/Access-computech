The Affordable Indian-Made POS System for Your Business

Indian businesses need a POS machine that’s specifically made in India for India.

Running a business is no easy task. Between managing sales, processing payments, and keeping everything running smoothly, there’s always something to do. But what if you had a tool that could take some of that load off your shoulders? That’s exactly what the A20 and A20 FP POS systems are here to do.

These devices aren’t just packed with smart features, they’re designed and made in India, keeping in mind the needs of Indian businesses. They’re affordable, easy to use, and built to make everyday operations smoother and faster. Let’s see how they can help your business grow.

Why Choose Indian-Made POS Systems?

You may be wondering, “Why should I go for a POS system made in India?” Well, look no further, here are some great reasons why you absolutely should:

  • Affordable

    One of the biggest advantages of choosing an Indian-made POS system is that it’s much more affordable than many international brands. You’re getting top-notch technology at a fraction of the price, which is especially great for small and medium businesses.

  • Made in India for Indian Businesses

    These systems are designed with India’s unique business needs in mind. Whether it’s managing GST invoicing, accepting payments via UPI or QR codes, or ensuring your system is in sync with local regulations, these POS systems are built to fit perfectly into the Indian market.

  • Quick Support

    Since these systems are made locally, getting support or servicing your system is much easier. If something goes wrong, you don’t have to wait for days for a technician to arrive, local service means faster fixes and less downtime. Plus, with the option of remote diagnosis and service, issues can often be resolved quickly without the need for a technician to visit in person, saving you even more time and hassle.

A20 POS System

The A20 is the ideal POS system for businesses looking for something simple yet powerful.

Let’s break down how each feature works for you.

  • Easy-to-Use Android System

    The A20 runs on Android 10—think of it as a smartphone for your counter. It’s familiar, intuitive, and doesn’t need a tech expert to operate. Training your staff becomes super simple, saving a lot of time and effort.

  • Accepts Multiple Payment Methods

    Imagine a customer wants to pay with UPI, but your system only takes cards. Frustrating, right? The A20 solves this problem by accepting cards, UPI, QR codes, and NFC (contactless payments). This flexibility ensures you never lose a sale, no matter how your customers prefer to pay.

  • Stay Connected with Multiple Connectivity Options

    Whether your business is in a busy city or a remote area, the A20 ensures you’re always online. With Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and 4G connectivity, you can process payments and manage sales without interruptions. This feature is perfect for mobile setups like food trucks or events.

  • Fast and Quiet Printing

    The A20 includes a thermal printer, which is known for being super quick and quiet. Customers don’t have to wait long for their receipts, and the printer doesn’t make distracting noise during a busy sales rush. This little feature might seem simple, but it makes a big difference in keeping the customer experience smooth.

The A20 FP POS System

Need something more secure? the A20 FP is a step up. It takes everything that makes the A20 great and adds a few more powerful features that are perfect for businesses handling sensitive transactions.

  • Fingerprint Security

    The A20 FP comes with a built-in fingerprint scanner, approved by UIDAI for Aadhaar authentication. This means it’s secure, reliable, and trusted for handling identity-based transactions. Fingerprint access ensures only authorised staff can use the device, reducing the risk of fraud or unauthorised actions. Think of it as a smart lock for your business!

  • Built to Withstand Heavy Use

    The A20 FP is built tough for busy environments. Whether it’s a restaurant during peak hours or a high-traffic retail store, this device performs consistently. It’s designed to handle dust and water, making it reliable even in challenging conditions. Plus, its shock-proof design ensures it survives accidental falls from counters or tables.

  • Connectivity That Works Everywhere

    The A20 FP offers multiple connectivity options like USB 2.0, Micro USB, and Type-C ports. Whether you’re at a fixed location or need portability, this device ensures seamless connectivity for uninterrupted operations.

Why the A20 and A20 FP are a Great Fit for Your Business

These POS systems are not just about accepting payments, they’re about making your entire sales process easier.

Here’s a quick recap of why you should consider them:

  • User-Friendly: Both systems are super easy to use, meaning you don’t need to worry about complicated training.
  • Compact Design: Their small size means they don’t take up much space on your counter, perfect for smaller shops or crowded counters.
  • Future-Ready: They’re built on Android, so they’re ready to handle new updates and features, ensuring they’re useful for years to come.
  • Portable: The A20 and A20 FP are lightweight and easy to carry, making them ideal for mobile businesses. Whether you need to move the POS between locations or take it to different areas within your store, their portability ensures you can stay flexible and efficient.
Supporting Local Innovation

By choosing the A20 or A20 FP, you’re not just getting a reliable POS system, you’re also supporting local Indian innovation. These systems are designed and built to meet the needs of Indian businesses, and by using them, you’re helping fuel the growth of local tech industries.

Get Your Business Upgraded with A20 and A20 FP

Both the A20 and A20 FP POS systems are great choices for businesses of all sizes. Whether you need a simple system to manage payments or a more secure setup to protect sensitive information, these systems have got you covered.
