Access Computech Wins Exim Club's Best IT Company of the Year 2024 Award

Access Computech Pvt. Ltd. wins big at Western India Exim Awards 2024, recognised for growth, customer satisfaction, and excellence in innovation and technology

Access Computech Pvt. Ltd. (ACPL), a leading provider of Made-in-India POS machines, biometric systems, access control systems, and attendance recording systems, has been awarded the prestigious "Best IT Company of the Year 2024" by Exim Club.

The accolade was presented during the Western India Exim Awards 2024 ceremony held on January 7, 2025, at Narayani Heights, Ahmedabad.

Upon receiving the award, Mr. Ajay Sinha, Founder & Managing Director at ACPL, remarked, "This recognition is a testament to our team's dedication to delivering superior technology solutions and exceptional after-sales service. We are honored to be acknowledged by Exim Club and remain committed to driving innovation in the IT sector."

“ACPL's commitment to excellence is evident through its competitive pricing, superior technology, and immediate assistance, setting it apart in the industry,” added Mr. Shaurya Singh Katal, VP Sales & Marketing at ACPL.

Mr. G J Brahmbhatt, President of Exim Club, emphasised the significance of the awards, stating, "Our club has long been a catalyst for growth, nurturing vital business relationships, and cultivating camaraderie among its distinguished members."

Adding to this momentous achievement, ACPL was also recently recognised as one of the 10 Most Promising Payment & Merchant Services in 2024 by Finance Outlook India.

For more information about Access Computech Pvt. Ltd. and its range of products, please visit https://www.accesscomputech.com/.

About Access Computech Pvt. Ltd.

Access Computech Pvt. Ltd. (ACPL) is a Vadodara-based IT company specialising in the development and distribution of Made-in-India POS machines, biometric systems, access control systems, and attendance recording systems. With a focus on superior technology and exceptional after-sales service, ACPL caters to a diverse clientele across various industries.

Our mission is simple: To consistently meet and exceed the expectations of our clients by adhering to the highest industry standards.

About Exim Club

Exim Club, established in 1994, is a prominent association dedicated to serving the interests of exporters and importers by providing solutions and advocating for business advancement, economic growth, and job creation.
