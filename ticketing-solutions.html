Ticketing Solutions

End-to-End Fare Collection and Access Management

Access Computech’s Ticketing Solutions provide a complete platform for managing fare collection, access control, and reporting across multiple environments. Combined with Access Computech’s POS and biometric devices, it delivers reliable, transparent, and automated ticketing for both open and closed systems.

What Ticketing
Solutions Do

The platform streamlines ticketing and access workflows, replacing manual logs and paper tickets with a digital ecosystem:

Supports transit, parking, canteens, events, campuses, and gated communities

Works online or offline, syncing automatically when connectivity is available

Integrates seamlessly with ACPL POS, validators, QR scanners, printers, and biometric or card-based access

Automates fare rules, passes, discounts, prepaid balances, and subscriptions with full audit trails

Provides detailed reporting and reconciliation for collections, trips, and operator performance

Key Features

Multi-Vertical Coverage: Supports public transport, parking lots, canteens, tolls, cinemas, events, and private campuses.

Integrated Hardware: Works with our POS terminals, barcode/QR scanners, printers, and optional biometric/card-based access.

Flexible Fare & Rule Engine: Configurable distance, time, zone, and slab-based fares; supports discounts, passes, prepaid balances, and subscriptions.

Advanced Capture Options: Barcode, QR, and number plate scanning enable automated entry/exit for vehicles and passengers.

Online & Offline Operation: Designed for Indian field conditions with intermittent connectivity.

Reporting & Reconciliation: Trip, canteen, and event sales reports; shift-wise settlements; operator-wise performance; and payment partner reconciliation.

Where Ticketing Solutions Excel

Public transportation systems and shuttle services
Event entry and venue access
Parking management & toll collections
Canteen management in campuses and corporate facilities

Designed to
Work Together

Ticketing Solutions integrate with other Access Computech systems for a unified payment and access ecosystem:

Soundbox Server – Provides instant transaction notifications and confirmations

Banks & Aggregators – Ensures reconciliation and integration with financial institutions

SmartPay & PayNext – Works with L3-certified payment applications for secure, certified transactions

Why Access Computech

With real-world deployments across transit, parking, and canteen systems, Access Computech provides solutions built for Indian field conditions. The platform is scalable, reliable, and fully auditable, giving operators full visibility and control.
