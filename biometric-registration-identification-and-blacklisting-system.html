Biometric Registration,
Identification & Blacklisting System

One person. One biometric identity. Enforced across companies and systems.

BRIBS is a biometric-based identity management system that ensures every individual is registered once and only once. It prevents duplicate enrolments, identity misuse, and unauthorised re-entry by relying on unalterable biometric traits instead of cards, numbers, or documents.

This ensures identity integrity not just within a single system, but across locations and organisations participating in the BRIBS network.

Biometric-First
Identity Management

BRIBS identifies individuals using unalterable biometric characteristics, including

Fingerprint recognition

Iris recognition

Facial recognition

Multi-modal biometric combinations

These biometric traits form a permanent identity profile that cannot be shared, forged, or altered, removing dependency on cards, ID numbers, or physical documents.

BRIBS also supports cross-company blacklist visibility.
If an individual has been blacklisted by a participating organisation, that status is visible during enrolment at another organisation within the BRIBS network — preventing re-entry under alternate credentials.

How BRIBS
Performs at Scale

BRIBS is built to operate reliably across large and growing biometric databases.

Key capabilities include

Large-scale biometric comparison across thousands or millions of records

High matching accuracy, even with partial or low-quality biometric captures

Fast processing for real-time identification and verification

Centralised blacklist enforcement across connected deployments
